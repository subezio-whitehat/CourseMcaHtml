KOTTAYAM DETAILS

Kottayam (കോട്ടയം)is a city in the Indian state of Kerala. Flanked by the Western Ghats on the east
and the Vembanad Lake and paddy fields of Kuttanad on the west, Kottayam is a place that is known for extraordinary qualities.
Kottayam is located in the basin of the Meenachil River at an average elevation of 3 metres (9.8 ft) above sea level,
and has a moderate climate.
