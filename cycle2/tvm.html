THIRUVANANTHAPURAM DETAILS

Thiruvananthapuram,commonly known by its former name Trivandrum, is the capital of the Indian state of Kerala. Located on the west coast of India near the extreme south of the mainland, Thiruvananthapuram is a major information technology hub in Kerala and contributes 55% of the state's software exports as of 2016. Referred to by Mahatma Gandhi as the "Evergreen city of India",the city is characterised by its undulating terrain of low coastal hills.
